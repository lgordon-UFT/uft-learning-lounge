Part 1 — Role

Who the AI should be. Assigning a role tells the model which knowledge and voice to draw on — it focuses the output the same way an expert focuses on their specialty.

Why a role sharpens output

🎯

Narrows a giant general model down to the perspective you need.

🗣️

Sets the vocabulary and tone automatically — a lawyer and a coach write very differently.

📐

Raises the assumed expertise, so you get advice, not generic info.

✍️

Usually the first sentence of your prompt: "You are a..."

✅ EXAMPLE ROLE

"You are an experienced NYC public-school chapter leader who knows the UFT contract and speaks plainly to busy teachers."

💡 TRY THESE ROLES

• a veteran 4th-grade teacher
• a school communications director
• a patient instructional coach
• a union grievance specialist
