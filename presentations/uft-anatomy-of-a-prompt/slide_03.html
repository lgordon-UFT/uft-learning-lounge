Why Your Prompt Matters

Garbage in, garbage out. The AI can only work with what you give it — your prompt is the steering wheel. A vague ask gets a vague answer.
🚗
❌

Weak & Vague

"Write something about our parent-teacher night."

What you get back:

  • Generic, could be any school
  • Wrong length and wrong tone
  • Missing the date, time, and details
  • You rewrite it all yourself anyway
✅

Clear & Specific

"Write a warm, 120-word email to parents inviting them to Parent-Teacher Night on Oct 24 at 6pm in the gym. Mention free childcare. 6th-grade reading level."

What you get back:

  • On-message and on-brand
  • Right length, right tone
  • All the key details included
  • Ready to send with a quick glance

Same AI. Same task. The only thing that changed was the prompt.
