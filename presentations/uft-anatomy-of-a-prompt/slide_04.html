The 4-Part Prompt Framework

Every strong prompt is built from four simple building blocks. Master these and you can ask any AI for anything:

🎭
PART 1

Role

Who the AI should be. Give it an identity and expertise.

🗂️
PART 2

Context

What it needs to know. The background, audience, constraints.

🎯
PART 3

Task

What it should do. One clear, specific ask.

📋
PART 4

Format

How you want it delivered. Shape, length, and tone.

Role + Context + Task + Format = a prompt that actually works.
