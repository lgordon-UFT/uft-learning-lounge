Part 3 — Task

The clear, specific ask. Lead with one strong action verb and name a concrete deliverable. If you're vague here, no amount of role or context will save the output.

✨ Strong verbs: Draft • Summarize • Compare • Rewrite • Outline • List

❌

Weak Task

"Help me with the newsletter."

Help how? Write it? Edit it? Brainstorm topics? The AI has to guess — and it usually guesses wrong.

✅

Strong Task

"Draft the opening paragraph for our chapter newsletter announcing the new mentoring program."

One verb (draft), one deliverable (an opening paragraph), one clear subject. Nothing to guess.
