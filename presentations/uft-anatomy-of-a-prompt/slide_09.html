ChatGPT vs Claude vs Gemini

The same 4-part prompt works in all three. They're more alike than different — here's where each tends to shine for UFT staff:

💬

ChatGPT

by OpenAI

Best at

The versatile all-rounder. Great for brainstorming, drafting, and quick everyday tasks. Huge add-on ecosystem.

Free access

Solid free tier at chatgpt.com; paid plan unlocks the newest model.

Good for: a daily go-to for emails, ideas, and first drafts.

🖋️

Claude

by Anthropic

Best at

Long documents and careful writing. Strong at reading big PDFs and producing thoughtful, natural-sounding prose.

Free access

Free tier at claude.ai with daily limits; paid plan for heavier use.

Good for: summarizing the contract or polishing a long memo.

💎

Gemini

by Google

Best at

Living inside Google Workspace — Docs, Gmail, Drive — plus fast, current web research.

Free access

Free at gemini.google.com with any Google account.

Good for: staff who live in Google Docs and Gmail all day.

Don't overthink it — pick one, learn it well, and reuse the same 4-part prompt everywhere.
