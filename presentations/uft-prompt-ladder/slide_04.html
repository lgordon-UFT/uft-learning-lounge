Your Challenge

Write the simplest possible version of your task as a prompt.

One sentence. No explanations. No role. No format. Just ask for the thing. This is how most people start — and why results are often generic.

💡 Example Scenarios by Role

Membership: "Write a membership renewal email."
Communications: "Write a social media post about the contract."
PD / Training: "Write a workshop agenda."
Grievance: "Explain what a grievance is."

⏱️ Timer: 5 Minutes

What to do:

  1. Write your ONE-sentence prompt
  2. Paste it into ChatGPT or Claude
  3. Read the response — notice how generic it is
  4. Paste your prompt + response to the Padlet (label: YOUR NAME — Level 1)

💬 Reflection question (hold your answer for debrief)

"Does this response feel like it was written FOR you — or for anyone?"
