How to Play

1

Pick Your Scenario

Think of ONE real task from your job — something you actually do. You will use this same task for all 4 rounds.

2

Complete Each Level

Each round has a challenge card telling you what NEW element to add to your prompt. You build on the previous round — never start over.

3

Paste to the Shared Board

After each round, paste your final prompt AND the AI response into the shared Padlet. Label it with your name and level.

4

Vote & Win

At the end, the group reads the Level 4 prompts on the board and votes for the BEST AI response. Most points + most votes wins!

📊 Point System

🪜 Level 1 — Basic10 pts
🪜 Level 2 — Intermediate25 pts
🪜 Level 3 — Advanced40 pts
🌟 Level 4 — Expert50 pts + BONUS votes

⚡ Bonus Points

+5 pts if your Level 4 response is voted Best by the group • +5 pts if you finish all 4 levels before time is called • +5 pts if you try it in BOTH ChatGPT AND Claude
