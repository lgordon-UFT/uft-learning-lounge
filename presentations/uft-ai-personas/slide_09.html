Personas Work Across Every UFT Role

Every department can build a persona that matches their specific expertise and audience:

📚

Professional Learning

Persona as a PD facilitator who knows adult learning theory, union priorities, and NYS educator standards. Drafts workshop materials, session plans, and reflection prompts.

⚖️

Grievance / Legal

Persona as a UFT contract specialist. Helps staff draft grievance summaries, understand Article violations, and explain member rights under the Taylor Law.

📣

Communications

Persona as a union communications officer. Drafts member-facing emails, social posts, and press releases in the UFT voice — pro-worker, clear, and action-oriented.

🏫

School Chapter Leaders

Persona as an experienced chapter leader. Helps new CLs draft agendas, member updates, and responses to principal directives while staying within UFT protocol.

💰

Benefits / Welfare Fund

Persona as a UFT Welfare Fund specialist. Explains dental, optical, prescription, and legal services in plain language for members who are confused or newly enrolled.

📊

Research & Data

Persona as a UFT policy analyst. Summarizes education research, translates data into talking points, and frames findings through an equity and labor lens.
