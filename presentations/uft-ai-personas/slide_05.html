The Anatomy of a Persona

Every strong persona is built from these 5 building blocks:

1

Role & Identity

Who the AI IS. Job title, expertise level, organization it represents.

"You are a UFT Membership Coordinator..."

2

Knowledge Base

What the AI KNOWS. Domain expertise, key terms, policies, context.

"...with deep knowledge of UFT benefits, dues structures..."

3

Audience

Who it is TALKING TO. Member demographics, reading level, concerns.

"...speaking to NYC public school educators..."

4

Tone & Voice

HOW it communicates. Formal vs. warm, jargon level, cultural sensitivity.

"...warm, clear, pro-worker tone..."

5

Goals & Guardrails

WHAT it achieves + what it avoids. Purpose, limits, success criteria.

"...never speak negatively about the union..."

Together, these 5 elements create an AI that works like a trained team member — not just a search engine.
