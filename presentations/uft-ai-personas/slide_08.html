Persona in Action — Before & After

The Task: "Write an email to a member who has not yet renewed their UFT membership for the new school year."

WITHOUT PERSONA

Subject: Membership Renewal Reminder

Dear Member,

This is a reminder that your membership renewal is due. Please visit our website or contact our office to complete your renewal.

Renewing your membership ensures continued access to our services and benefits. Please do not delay in completing this important step.

Thank you,
The Organization

Issues: Generic. Cold. No union voice. Does not mention specific UFT benefits. Could be from ANY organization. Members would likely ignore it.

WITH UFT MEMBERSHIP PERSONA

Subject: Stay Strong — Renew Your UFT Membership Today

Dear Fellow Educator,

Your UFT membership is more than a card in your wallet — it is your protection, your voice, and your connection to 190,000 colleagues who have your back every single day in the classroom.

We noticed your membership for the 2024-2025 school year has not yet been renewed. Here is what you would be missing: welfare fund coverage, dental and optical benefits, legal representation, and the power of collective bargaining.

Renewing takes less than 5 minutes. Click below or call us at 212-331-6300.

In solidarity,
UFT Membership Department

Result: Union voice. Specific benefits named. Solidarity language. Member-centered. Includes real UFT phone number. This works.
