Why Use Personas?

🚫

Problem 1: Context Loss

Every new chat starts from zero. The AI does not know you are a UFT membership coordinator, what your members need, or how your union communicates.

"Why does it keep giving me corporate HR answers when I need union answers?"

🔄

Problem 2: Constant Re-Explaining

Without a persona, you spend the first 5 prompts of every session explaining who you are, who your audience is, and what tone to use.

"I waste half my time just setting up the conversation."

✅

Solution: The Persona

Paste your persona once at the start of a session. The AI immediately knows its role, your context, your audience, and your goals — instantly.

"Now I get relevant union-focused answers from prompt one."

Key Insight: A persona is a one-time investment that pays off every single session. Build it once — reuse it forever.
