Persona vs. Prompt — What Is the Difference?

📝

A PROMPT

A single question or instruction

►

Task-specific — used once

►

Tells AI WHAT to do

►

Results vary each session

►

Like a single work order

"Write me a letter about open enrollment."
+
Work best
together
🧑‍💻

A PERSONA

A standing identity for the AI

►

Reusable — set once per session

►

Tells AI WHO it is

►

Ensures consistent results every time

►

Like an employee orientation

"You are a UFT Membership expert who helps educators navigate their benefits..."

Pro tip: Set your persona FIRST, then ask your prompts. The persona is the foundation — every prompt builds on top of it.
